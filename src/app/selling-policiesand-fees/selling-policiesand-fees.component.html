Selling Practice Policies and Fees
At Bydd, our mission is to keep online selling simple, affordable and fair for all. In order to fulfil this mission, there are a few requirements and expectations for sellers to follow.

Listing Policy
1. While in the development stages, the current categories allowed on Bydd are restricted to numismatic and related items. Categories for other collectibles will be coming soon.

2. All items listed must be in the possession of the seller. Bydd does not allow proxy listings.

3. All effort must be made to ensure accurate photos and descriptions.

Seller Standards Policy
1. To be able to sell on Bydd without restriction, sellers must have a minimum of 85% positive feedback.

2. Sellers with more than 5 active disputes will temporarily be unable to list until the disputes have been satisfactorily mediated

Fees and Payout
Bydd charges a flat 8.5% final value fee, based on the amount of the sale. The total amount is defined as just the item price, not including handling, shipping, tax or any other applicable fees.

1. Buyers pay the transaction fees associated with the sales price. The payment is then held by Bydd as escrow. Once a tracking number of other proof of shipment is uploaded onto the Seller’s Hub, the payment is then sent to the seller.

2. Sellers are responsible for associated transaction fees as payment is sent from Bydd to the seller’s account. For a no-fees option, we recommend Bank Transfer.

3. All applicable taxes and government fees are the responsibility of the seller. Bydd cannot offer tax advice. For specific tax policies and best practices, sellers are encouraged to contact a tax authority or agent in their locality.

4. Shipment Policy

5. Sellers are strongly encouraged to ship their items within 3 business days of receipt of payment and are required to ship their items no more than 5 business days of receipt of payment.

6. Sellers are required to upload tracking number or other proof of payment onto the Seller’s hub to receive their payout
