How Bydd Works
Bydd utilizes a unique Dutch Auction selling format to provide a superior marketplace experience for both buyers and sellers. Buying and selling on Bydd is quick, simple, and intuitive. A Dutch auction is a type of auction where the auctioneer starts with the highest asking price, which lowers over time until a participant accepts the price. The first bid made is the winning bid, ensuring that the buyer pays the fairest price they are willing to offer.

Dutch auctions are especially beneficial for goods of high value or unique items, where the true market value might be unknown. They are also resistant to fraud and less time-consuming than traditional auction formats.

The Benefits of Dutch Auctions on Bydd

For Buyers:

• Fair Pricing: You only pay what you're willing to pay, not a cent more.
• Instant Purchases: No need to wait for a prolonged bidding period.
• Transparency: The process is straightforward, reducing the risk of fraud.


For Sellers:

• Maximised Potential Final Price: The first bid accepted is often the highest the market is willing to pay.
• Reduced Risk of Unsuccessful Sale: The price lowers until a buyer is found, ensuring a sale as long as the reserve price is met.
• Efficiency: Faster sales process compared to traditional auctions.
